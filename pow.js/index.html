pow.js — Algorithmic sunburst generator via CSS, canvas and jQuery.

March 28, 2012 — This jQuery plugin paints a burst pattern onto your element of choice, and is freely available under the MIT license. Pow.js generates the pattern in canvas, and simply layers in the resulting image data as a CSS background. It's kinda actually not that complicated.
